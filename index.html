Hands-On Robot Design and 3D Printing

Hands-On Robot Design and 3D Printing

Children design and 3D print all the components of a hand-cranked flashlight. The flashlight features a turning handle that spins a motor, generating electricity

Electronics and Circuits

Electronics and Circuits

Kids learn how to convert the electricity generated by the crank into light. They explore simple electronic components and construct various circuits using these components
